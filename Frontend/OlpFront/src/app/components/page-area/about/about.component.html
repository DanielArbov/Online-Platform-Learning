About the Platform

Welcome to our Online Learning Platform! Our platform is designed to enhance your learning experience with easy access to courses and lessons. We offer two types of users:

Instructors

As an instructor, you have the ability to create and manage courses and lessons. You can add detailed descriptions, resources, and set up assignments to guide your students through their learning journey.

Students

As a student, you can enroll in courses, track your progress, and access all lessons at any time. The platform allows you to monitor your progress through each course and lesson, helping you understand what you've completed and what you still need to finish.

This platform is built to make learning interactive and personalized for each user. Join today and start your learning journey!
